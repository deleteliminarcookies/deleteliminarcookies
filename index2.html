Eliminar cookies

Para eliminar todas las cookies, sigue las instrucciones para tu navegador:

  • Google Chrome: Copia y pega en la barra de direcciones: chrome://settings/clearBrowserData
  • Mozilla Firefox: Copia y pega en la barra de direcciones: about:preferences#privacy
  • Safari: Preferencias del sistema y busca privacidad para encontrar la configuración de cookies.
  • Microsoft Edge: Copia y pega en la barra de direcciones: edge://settings/clearBrowserData

Delete Cookies

Delete cookies

To delete all cookies, follow the instructions for your browser:

  • Google Chrome: Copy and paste in the address bar: chrome://settings/clearBrowserData
  • Mozilla Firefox: Copy and paste in the address bar: about:preferences#privacy
  • Safari: System Preferences and look for Privacy to find the cookie settings.
  • Microsoft Edge: Copy and paste in the address bar: edge://settings/clearBrowserData
